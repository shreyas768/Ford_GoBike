INTRODUCTION¶

This project Ford GoBike dataset of Year 2019 (till July). GoBike is the Bay Area's new bike share system, with thousands of public bikes for use across the greater San Francisco Bay area. An app was also created to co-piloting for getting around town. For more information on this program, please go to https://www.fordgobike.com. Our analysis covers data from Jan 2019 to July 2019. The above mentioned Data Set focuses on information with regard to the individual rides undertaken in a bike-sharing system expanded over the greater San Francisco Bay Area. We will need to join together multiple data files. As a first step, we will import relevant libraries.¶

We will try to answer some questions and get insights, comparing performances that depends on various factors. For Example:-¶
  1. When are most trips taken in terms of time of day, day of the week, or month of the year?
  2. How long (duration) does the average trip take?
  3. Which is a more popular ride coming from, a subscriber or customer?
  4. People of which age group takes most rides?
  5. How does number of trips vary in a particular day
